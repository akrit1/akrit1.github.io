About

Fanalytics brings you weekly positional rankings supported by calculation, not conjecture. Our sophisticated machine-learning algorithms use advanced analytics, including weather data, opponent strength, box score trends, and more. The code for this website and the computation behind rankings can be found here 📊

If you find yourself struggling week-to-week in setting your lineup, leave the hard decisions up to us. Use our predictions to help you decide which players to start, bench, and cut each week. The numbers speak for themselves, and Fanalytics is ringing - it's up to you to pick up the phone ☎️

Fanalytics is brought to you by Akrit Sinha, a data scientist in the USA with a passion for sports analytics 🏈
